about me

- fueled by fresh air, rad people, and a plant-based diet -

Hello! I'm Dorothy. I am 19 years old, born in Vancouver, Canada and raised in Virginia. I am currently attending college - studying Computer Science & Media Studies. I have been dabbling in photography and design for a couple of years now -- from freelancing with brands and companies I find through social media such as Instagram to working through 99designs. I draw inspiration from the world around me.

Favorite quote atm: "Be where your feet are."

Marketing Student Assistant at UVA

I work as a graphic designer and social media content developer for the Office of Dean of Students at UVA. Managing the @UVAODOS Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, and Vine has taught me how to consolidate my own design eye and preferences with a brand image.
